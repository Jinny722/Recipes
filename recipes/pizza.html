Pizza

Description:

Pizza is a type of flatbread that is typically topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings such as meats, vegetables, and herbs. It is baked in an oven and is a popular dish in many countries around the world. Pizza can be made in a variety of styles, including thin-crust, thick-crust, and stuffed crust, and can be customized to suit individual tastes.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 1/4 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/4 cup sliced bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup sliced onions
  • 1/4 cup sliced olives
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 450°F (230°C). If using a pizza stone, place it in the oven while preheating.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness and shape.
  3. Transfer the dough to a baking sheet or pizza peel lined with parchment paper.
  4. Spread the tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a border around the edges for the crust.
  5. Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese over the sauce, followed by the Parmesan cheese.
  6. Arrange the pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, and olives on top of the cheese.
  7. Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and melted.
  8. Remove the pizza from the oven and sprinkle the chopped basil over the top.
  9. Slice the pizza and serve hot. Enjoy!

Enjoy your meal!
